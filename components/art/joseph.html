Joseph Beuys: From the Secret Block to Rosc

Print

Hugh Lane Gallery will mark the Joseph Beuys centenary in 2021 with a focused exhibition looking back on the artist’s relationship to Ireland. It will include the three blackboards that Beuys created in the Gallery in October 1974 while giving a lecture during the exhibition, The Secret Block for a Secret Person in Ireland.
