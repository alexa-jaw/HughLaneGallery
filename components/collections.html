PrintsCecil King (1921-1986):
Present in Time Future

Cecil King had a long and rich association with the Hugh Lane Gallery.
His first retrospective was held here in 1981.
It is therefore with great pleasure that we present King’s work from the collection to mark the 100th anniversary of his birth.

Sean Scully Room

As a result of an ongoing collaboration with Sean Scully,
the gallery is honoured to be the recipient of a gift of eight paintings
presented by the artist in 2006 and Landline Gray, donated in 2018.

The Lane Legacy

The Lane Legacy exhibition, which was launched by Uachtarán na hÉireann, Michael D Higgins on 20 January 2020,
celebrates the gallery’s founder, the philanthropist Sir Hugh Lane, as well as the gallery’s 112th anniversary.
His first retrospective was held here in 1981. It is therefore with great pleasure
that we present King’s work from the collection to mark the 100th anniversary of his birth.



Logan Sisley
